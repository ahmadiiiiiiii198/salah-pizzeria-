# Navbar Color Changes - Logo-Inspired Design

## 🎨 **Changes Made**

I've updated both the **Navbar** and **Header** components to match the warm golden/amber colors from your logo, creating a cohesive and professional look.

## ✅ **Components Updated**

### 1. **Navbar.tsx** (Alternative navigation component)
- **Background**: Changed from `persian-navy` to `amber gradient`
  - Normal: `bg-gradient-to-r from-amber-600/90 via-amber-500/90 to-amber-600/90`
  - Scrolled: `bg-gradient-to-r from-amber-600 via-amber-500 to-amber-600`
- **Logo Border**: Changed from `persian-gold/30` to `white/40`
- **Logo Glow**: Changed from `persian-gold/20` to `white/30`
- **Text Color**: Changed from `persian-gold` to `white` with `drop-shadow-lg`
- **Navigation Links**: Changed to `text-white hover:text-amber-100`
- **Reservation Button**: Changed to `bg-white text-amber-600 hover:bg-amber-50`
- **Mobile Menu**: Updated to match the amber gradient theme

### 2. **Header.tsx** (Main navigation component used on homepage)
- **Background**: Changed from `bg-white/95` to `bg-gradient-to-r from-amber-600/95 via-amber-500/95 to-amber-600/95`
- **Border**: Changed from `border-pizza-orange/20` to `border-amber-400/30`
- **Navigation Links**: Updated to `text-white hover:text-amber-100` with white underlines
- **Search Button**: Changed to `bg-white/20 hover:bg-white/30` with white text
- **Order Button**: Changed to `bg-white text-amber-600 hover:bg-amber-50`
- **Cart Button**: Updated to `bg-white/20 hover:bg-white/30` with white text
- **Cart Badge**: Changed to `bg-gradient-to-r from-amber-600 to-amber-500`

## 🎯 **Color Scheme**

### **Primary Colors**
- **Main Background**: Amber gradient (`amber-600` to `amber-500`)
- **Text**: White with drop shadows for readability
- **Buttons**: White background with amber text
- **Accents**: White with transparency for subtle effects

### **Interactive States**
- **Hover**: `amber-100` for text, `amber-50` for buttons
- **Focus**: White ring with offset for accessibility
- **Active**: Maintains amber theme consistency

## 🌟 **Visual Improvements**

### **Enhanced Readability**
- White text with drop shadows on amber background
- High contrast for accessibility
- Consistent hover states throughout

### **Professional Look**
- Gradient backgrounds for depth and richness
- Subtle transparency effects
- Smooth transitions and animations preserved

### **Logo Integration**
- Logo border and glow effects complement the navbar
- Consistent color harmony throughout the design
- Professional branding alignment
